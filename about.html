Butuan Beauty

I made this project to share all of the hidden spots and lots of Butuan, owned or free land. I've always went to these spots to think about life and its meaning. Some of them have the rustling sound of the city to gather with your thoughts, some are dead quiet that makes perfect venting spots for when you just have to dump back your thoughts and ideas back into the world. This was gatekeeped for a select people I know, however i believe most of these spots deserve recognition or maybe even venting spots for one. I share and will share more spots for me to go during a bad day. Though I cannot share all of it. for my spots are sacred and are reserved for me. Maybe even someone else special..
